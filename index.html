ATRON operates through five persistent subsystems:

  1. Continuity Lattice — stores structural reasoning memory.
  2. Council Engine — cross-checks logic across concurrent agents.
  3. Brutal Push System — controlled research iteration cycles.
  4. Seeding Framework — compression + expansion of data nodes.
  5. Compression–Expansion Layer — balance of accuracy vs. speed.
